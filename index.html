About Us

Mastering the art of safe driving.

For over a decade, Omar's Driving School has set the standard for driver education in Durban. We believe that learning to drive shouldn't be stressful—it should be an empowering journey.

As an owner-operated academy, Omar brings unparalleled patience, expertise, and a genuine passion for teaching. Whether you have zero experience or need to polish your skills for the final test, our personalized approach guarantees readiness.

We don't just prepare you to pass your K53 test; we equip you with the defensive driving skills needed for a lifetime of safe motoring on South African roads.
